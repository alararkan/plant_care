# 🌿 Planties

A personal plant care app for our apartment in Bremen.
Track watering, repotting, and get live weather for our plants.

## 📱 Add to your iPhone

1. Open Safari on your iPhone
2. Go to: **https://alararkan.github.io/plant_care**
3. Tap the **Share** button (box with arrow at the bottom)
4. Tap **"Add to Home Screen"**
5. Tap **Add** — done!

## Features

- 🌤 Live Bremen weather with plant-specific alerts
- 💧 Watering log per plant shared in real time
- 🪴 Repotting log per plant
- 🗓 Next watering date tracker
- 🌿 All plants with botanical illustrations and care info
